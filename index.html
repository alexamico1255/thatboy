Thatboythrift is an exceptional black-owned and operated online business that has established itself as a prominent destination for vintage fashion enthusiasts. With a keen eye for style and a passion for sustainability, ThatBoyThrift offers a carefully curated selection of timeless garments that are sure to make a statement.

Behind the success of ThatBoyThrift is its visionary founder, Mr. Tyrese Wilson. With a profound appreciation for fashion and a dedication to promoting sustainable practices, Tyrese has curated an impressive collection of vintage clothing from different eras, ensuring that each piece is not only fashionable but also embodies a rich history.

Despite being an online business, ThatBoyThrift has made its mark in the physical realm as well. With a consistent presence at Soda City, a renowned marketplace for local vendors, the brand has forged connections with its community and garnered a loyal customer base. Additionally, ThatBoyThrift's dedication to quality and style has earned them a spot on the prestigious depop Top 100 list, solidifying their position as a leader in the vintage fashion industry.

Embracing the principles of sustainability and inclusivity, ThatBoyThrift is not just a business but a movement. By championing vintage fashion, Tyrese and his team strive to inspire a sense of individuality, encourage conscious consumerism, and celebrate the rich heritage of fashion. With ThatBoyThrift, you can discover the perfect vintage piece that not only looks remarkable but also carries a story worth sharing.
